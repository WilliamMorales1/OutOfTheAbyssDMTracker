# The Pudding King Boss Fight

## The Arena (Area 28–29)

Roughly 70 ft diameter cavern, ceiling 30+ ft, uneven floor.

- **Slime channels**: grooves in the floor/walls feeding into 3–4 shallow pools (5 ft squares, difficult terrain). Lair actions use these as spawn/hazard points.
- **Side tunnels**: at least 3 approach tunnels - one to area 29 (gibbering mouther feeding on oozes; can be lured in as a wildcard, see below), one toward the wererat warrens, one toward the main city (deep gnome militia route). These are where reinforcements physically enter.
- **Allies**: Wererats of Clan Goldwhisker & Deep Gnome Militia both fighting oozes and slimes side-by-side in the background.

## Phase 2 — True Form (Avatar of Juiblex) (show image of Juiblex)

Pudding King keeps all stats while adding/changing the following:
type = huge ooze
HP = 195
Legendary Resistance (3/Day)
Engulf (see gelatinous cube)
Legendary Action: Command one other ooze to use it's reaction to immediately attack
Lair Action at the start of every 3rd round:

- Acid Geyser. A point within 60 ft erupts in a 20-ft-radius burst. DC 15 Dex save, 2d6 acid damage (half on success); the area becomes difficult terrain slicked with ichor.
- Ceiling Ichor. Slime rains in a 30-ft radius centered on a creature of the King's choice. DC 13 Con save or blinded until the end of that creature's next turn.

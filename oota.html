⚔️ Out of the Abyss

DM Tracker

Out of the Abyss explores the Underdark, focusing on Abyssal Demon lords summoned by the drow Archmage Gromph Baenre under the manipulation of Lolth. Escaping the drow prison Velkynvelve, adventuring parties must navigate grueling challenges across subterranean settlements, including the duergar city Gracklstugh and the deep gnome enclave Blingdenstone. They face apocalyptic dangers posed by fiends like Demogorgon, Zuggtmoy, and Juiblex. Seeking answers within the stone giant library of Gravenhollow, the heroes subsequently flee to the surface city of Gauntlgrym to warn dwarf King Bruenor Battlehammer. Tasked with banishing the Demon lords back to the Abyss, the heroes ally with renegade drow archmage Vizeran DeVir. To achieve this, Vizeran devises a powerful ritual to draw the demons together. The heroes must ultimately travel to the dark elven city of Menzoberranzan to place a magical dark heart talisman and enact Vizeran DeVir's master plan, restoring lasting balance by forcing the fiends to violently destroy one another in an epic, combat scenario.
